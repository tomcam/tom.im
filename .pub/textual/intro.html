Retro is Really Back, Baby

By Tom Campbell | Staff reporter | Dec 14, 2020

(Dateline: Hollywoodland, CA) Is retro really back? "With a lot of producers, it's all about analog," says Seattle producer Mox Chehalis. "Not exclusively but it's one of the paints in my palette, for reals. I have a lot of vintage equipment because it just gives a feel you can't get any other way. God knows I've tried."
